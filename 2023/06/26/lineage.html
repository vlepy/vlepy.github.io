Lineage

The fall of customs destroys societies

See a vital lineage will never leave you alone with the trouble you got. Well lineages are large, organized families. They care about blood, wealth, heath, education, reputation of the power of the lineage. They do all they can to improve the lineage. Being a member of the lineage is beneficial, you have a lot of support from often rich and powerful people. Lineages have their rules, codex and leadership. It does not take much to start a lineage. But, it takes a lot of time to make the lineage big. Often few hundreds of years. Through that time you constantly have to prove and improve your lineage so it shows up an as glorious through the history.

They keep track of all the people they got, what they did, when they were born, died, etc. And by starting a lineage you get the founder status, you will be remembered as the founder of the lineage, as a great person. Lineages arrange marriage for young grown ups so everyone gets married in a good family. Dowry for a young woman is a great way to arrange them, every lineage wants their women to be married in good and wealthy families.

A good lineage will never leave you alone to die if they are able help. And it all starts with considering the general prosperity of your family is most important, when you realize you can make the name mean something. So yeah families values are a good virtue to have. Very good if you're conscious you're in a family and you should together with the family for mutual benefit. No we should make a lot of lineages. After 200 years it will be clear who takes the prime, who's the largest, strongest, most noble, smartest lineage of all lineages. And this lineage will take power in their hands. From the prime lineage there will be king chosen, the leader of the prime lineage. Head of all families in the nation. And everyone in the nation will say "hail to the king".

King could be a good guy. Like, I could go to the king if they let me and as him: "sire, i appreciate all of your magnificent country but we're a small group of Muslims and we can't afford to build a little mosque, could you build one for us?". And if king likes me and the idea he just gives an order. And they start building the mosque. So yea I'm big fan of lineage based societies.

Knowledge is very important in lineages. Like you learn stuff in a school lineage picks up. But you also carry knowledge about lineage itself. Generation after generation. And over time your lineage gets better and better. Like there are larger families helping each other but only in a formal lineage you can organize your family on a large scale and survive for long. And this makes things more clear, a situation when a distant member of the family rejects you can't happen. So anyway, those are wise families oriented to long term survival which is a virtue.

Elders live their late life with dignity. Kids get the best education possible. The blood improves.
You can live in New York and have lineage members all over your country, like in San Francisco or Chicago. So there is always someone to hang around with when you travel. After lineage grows you can have relatives in all kinds of places, military, medicine, corporations, police, cinematography, everywhere! Lineages are pure good, start one now!
